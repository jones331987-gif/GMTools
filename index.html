Leveling Guide — TSR 5.0

📖 How EXP Works

Characters earn experience through combat encounters. Exp is shared equally among all participating players.

Formula: (Tallied Exp × 2) ÷ Players, capped at 4 exp per player.

Each level requires 10 EXP. Overflow is wasted.

⚠ Leveling Milestones & Trials

Upon reaching levels 3, 6, and 9, characters must complete a Trial before they can earn further EXP. EXP gains halt until the trial is completed.

Warrior Trial

Protect NPC allies escorting them to safety under attack. Fail if any ally is downed.

Support Trial

Maintain a group of allies fighting hostile forces. Fail if any ally is downed.

Assassin Trial

Down high-priority targets before they down your party.

Destroyer Trial

Eliminate a swarm of enemies within a turn limit. Fail if downed or time runs out.

Disabler Trial

Break a boss's poise bar while protecting allies from high-damage threats.

📝 Logging EXP

  • Players log earned exp in the designated Discord channel.
  • A GM reacts with a ✅ check emoji once logged in the document.
  • The GM transfers exp to the public tracker doc.
  • Players do not self-manage their exp totals.
  • PvP exp is valid but farming is not tolerated.

💡 Quick Example

Party of 4 defeats: 3 Mooks (3 exp) + 1 Elite (2 exp) + 1 Boss (4 exp) = 9 raw exp.

Quest bonus: +1 = 10 total.

Formula: (10 × 2) ÷ 4 = 5 exp, but capped at 4 exp per player.

Each player gets 4 EXP.
